Portfolio

A glimpse of the projetcs I have worked on

Sales Order Analysis

Sales Order Analysis is created with date slicers, donuts to display aging due group, and cards to display total amounts and quantities. Sales comparison with the previous year is displayed on a clustered column chart. Top sales by customers, Top ordered items, and Top due orders are shown with a bar chart and the matrix table gives a detailed overview of due orders. Advanced DAX measures are used for sum, count, calculate, and time intelligence.

Data preparation and retrieval: MS SQL Server
ETL and Modeling: Power Query
BI Tool: Microsoft Power BI

Factory Operational Dashboard

Factory Operational Dashboard is proposed to give the whole operational status of a factory bounded in one page. It gives a glimpse of procurement, purchase of materials, consumption of materials, total production, and delivery in both quantities and amounts. With the donut visual purchase vs consumption, categorical productions, production vs deliveries are shown. The bar visual tells about the topmost purchases and deliveries by suppliers and customers. Matrix table gives a detailed view of production and deliveries by customer.

Data preparation and retrieval: MS SQL Server, MS Excel
ETL and Modeling: Power Query
BI Tool: Microsoft Power BI

TCPL Sales Analysis

TCPL Sales Analysis is a comprehensive dashboard that gives full insights into MTD sales conditions on various consumer products. MTD targets, gross sales, net sales, achievements, returns, and trends are visualized here. Different charts are used to visualize details on actual sales, cumulative sales, sales by zone, sales by zone, sales by person, targets, contributions, and achievements.

Data preparation and retrieval: MS SQL Server
Data Modeling: SSAS
BI Tool: QlikSense

Customer Analysis

ONC Customer Analysis is a dashboard that gives an analytical overview of customers of an Oncology Department of a renowned pharmaceutical in Bangladesh. Statuses of the total customer, active customer, inactive customer, new customer, customer coverage, sales, return, and outstanding are shown on top. Multiple categorial filters are also added on top for inquiry as needed. Month Wise Sales table shows detail on customer-wise sales. Customer-wise outstanding shows aging-wise outstanding and the percentages. A custom map visual is proposed to get a divisional customer count while scrolling through it. Different chart visualization used to differentiate the top purchasers, month-wise comparison with last year, Day wise analysis with last month, month-wise cumulative sales, aging-wise outstanding, sales vs outstanding, various categorical sales representation, top brand, and item analysis.

Data preparation and retrieval: Azure SQL
Data Modeling: SSAS
BI Tool: QlIkSense
